What is EcoSenz?

EcoSenz is a smart energy monitoring and management solution designed for residential homes and small commercial facilities. It provides real-time visibility into electricity consumption using a smart hub, clamp meters, and advanced analytics.

EcoSenz works with modern smart devices as well as legacy or non-smart equipment, making it a flexible and future-ready energy management platform. By combining cutting-edge technology with ease of use, EcoSenz empowers you to take control of your energy consumption and make informed decisions.
