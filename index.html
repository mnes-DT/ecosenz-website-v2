How EcoSenz Works

Simple setup, powerful insights — get started in four easy steps

01

Install

Plug in the EcoSenz Hub Smart Energy Monitor and attach optional clamp meters to your electrical panel. The system integrates seamlessly with your home’s power infrastructure for accurate energy measurement and load monitoring.

02

Connect

Use the EcoSenz App to pair devices via Matter, Zigbee, Thread, Wi-Fi, or Bluetooth, ensuring compatibility with   most smart home devices, IoT systems, and energy management solutions.

03

Analyze

Your energy consumption data is securely processed in the cloud using advanced real-time analytics and AI-driven energy insights. The system identifies usage patterns, peak load behavior, and energy inefficiencies.

Monitor & Optimize

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is energy monitoring important? ▼

Energy monitoring provides real-time visibility into electricity usage, helping identify inefficiencies, reduce waste, and lower utility bills.

Does EcoSenz require professional installation? ▼

EcoSenz is designed for simple setup. The hub is plug-and-play, and CT clamp meters can be installed quickly. Professional assistance may be recommended depending on your electrical panel configuration.

Does EcoSenz work during power outages? ▼

EcoSenz records and syncs data once power and connectivity are restored, ensuring continuity in your energy monitoring history.

Is EcoSenz compatible with utility smart meters? ▼

Yes. EcoSenz supports Zigbee Smart Energy Profile (SEP), making it compatible with many utility smart meters, subject to local utility configuration.

Is EcoSenz suitable for solar-powered homes? ▼

Yes. EcoSenz can monitor overall energy consumption and help homeowners better understand usage patterns alongside solar generation systems.
